  NEW DELHI: The Indian market is expected to open flat-to-higher on Tuesday tracking mixed trend seen in other Asian markets.     "The Nifty50 made a Bullish Belt Hold candle formation on the daily charts followed by a Bullish Hammer which indicates strength near support zones," Chandan Taparia, Derivatives & Technical Analyst - Equity Research at Anand Rathi Financial Services told ETMarkets.com.     "The Nifty50 has to surpass multiple hurdles at 8,686, 8,700 and then at 8,720 zones to start the next leg of rally towards 8,844 zones while on the downside multiple supports are seen at 8,550 then 8,500-8475 zones," he added.

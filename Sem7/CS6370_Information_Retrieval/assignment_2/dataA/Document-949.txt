Title : Mahindra Holidays & Resorts India Q4 net at Rs 32 crore - The Economic Times

  NEW DELHI: Mahindra Group firm Mahindra Holidays & Resorts India Ltd () today reported a standalone net profit of Rs 32.09 crore for the quarter ended March 31, 2016.     The company had posted a net profit after tax and minority interest of Rs 10.42 crore for the corresponding period of the previous fiscal, MHRIL said in a filing to BSE.     Standalone total income from operations of the company stood at Rs 253.99 crore for the quarter under consideration. It was Rs 203.92 crore for the same period year ago.     The company had posted a standalone net profit of Rs 117.34 crore for the fiscal year ended March 31, 2016. It was Rs 79.02 crore for the previous fiscal year.     Standalone total income from operations of the company stood at Rs 951.53 crore for the recently ended fiscal. It was Rs 794.85 crore for the year ago fiscal.     The results are not comparable, MHRIL said.     In a separate filing the company said its board has recommended dividend of Rs 5 per equity shares of Rs 10 each on 8,87,80,856 shares.     Shares of Mahindra Holidays & Resorts India today closed at Rs 420.60 per scrip on BSE, up 3.35 per cent from its previous close.
